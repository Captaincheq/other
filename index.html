2. Sea Freight

Out shipping network encompasses most major world’s seaport . We manage thousands of containers every year to and from Africa , Europe and South America . Our team is familiar with local customs laws and regulations- saving you time , money , and ensuring your sea freight shipments reach their destination safely. Springbok Delivery we’re not locked into strict sailing schedules. That gives us the freedom and flexibility to schedule your shipping on a variety os steamship lines , enables us to find the sailing time that best meets your shipments needs and budget. Because we can pick and choose carriers , we access more ports , more cost effective than vessel operating carriers. Springbok keeps an eye on freight schedules on your behalf , making ocean shipping as convenient as possible. We offer specialized handling , break bulk and Roll On Roll Off shipping, and we deal in all manner of sizes and weights for specialized cargo. Contact us with your specific requirements today.

3.Home delivery

Our home deliveries are flexible enough to handle high value and oversized deliveries. We pride ourselves on providing an exceptional customer experience at the time of delivery. With extra services like debris removal , room of choice assembly and more our team is equipped to meet all your needs. Levels of Home Delivery 1 Golden Hands - includes unpacking , delivery to room of choice and debris removal 2 Room of Choice - delivery to room of choice with packing left as is 3 Threshold- delivery inside the main entrance of the residence or garage 4 Standard - delivery to the first dry area outside the residence 5 Basic - notification of delivery, and the customer doesn’t need to schedule a delivery appointment
